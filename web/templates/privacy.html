Who we are

Suggested text: Our website address is: https://coupanhaven.com.

Comments

Suggested text: When visitors leave comments on our site, we collect the data provided in the comments form, along with the visitor's IP address and browser user agent string to help identify and prevent spam. An anonymized string created from your email address (also known as a hash) may be provided to the Gravatar service to check if you are using it. The Gravatar service's privacy policy can be found here: https://automattic.com/privacy/. After your comment is approved, your profile picture will be visible to the public in the context of your comment.

Media

Suggested text: If you upload images to our website, please ensure that these images do not contain embedded location data (EXIF GPS). Visitors to our website can download and extract location data from such images.

Cookies

Suggested text: If you leave a comment on Coupon Haven, you may opt-in to save your name, email address, and website in cookies. These cookies are for your convenience, so you don't have to re-enter your details when leaving another comment. These cookies will remain valid for one year.

When you visit our login page, a temporary cookie will be set to determine if your browser accepts cookies. This temporary cookie contains no personal data and is discarded when you close your browser.

Upon logging in, we will also set up several cookies to save your login information and screen display preferences. Login cookies remain valid for two days, while screen options cookies are retained for a year. If you select "Remember Me," your login will persist for two weeks. Logging out of your account will remove the login cookies.

If you edit or publish an article, an additional cookie will be saved in your browser. This cookie contains no personal data and solely indicates the post ID of the article you edited. It expires after 1 day.

Embedded content from other websites

Suggested text: Articles on Coupon Haven may include embedded content (such as videos, images, articles, etc.) from other websites. This embedded content behaves in the same manner as if you were visiting the originating website.

These external websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with the embedded content, including tracking your activity if you have an account and are logged in to that external website.

Who we share your data with

Suggested text: If you request a password reset on Coupon Haven, your IP address will be included in the reset email.

How long we retain your data

Suggested text: If you leave a comment, both the comment and its associated metadata will be retained indefinitely. This is done to automatically recognize and approve any follow-up comments, eliminating the need for them to go through a moderation queue.

For users who register on our website (if any), their personal information provided in their user profile will be stored. All users can view, edit, or delete their personal information at any time (with the exception of changing their username). Website administrators also have access to view and edit this information.

What rights you have over your data

Suggested text: If you have an account on Coupon Haven or have left comments, you can request to receive an exported file of your personal data held by us, including any data you've provided. You also have the right to request the erasure of any personal data we hold about you, with the exception of data we are obliged to keep for administrative, legal, or security purposes.

Where we send your data

Suggested text: Visitor comments may be subject to automated spam detection services.
